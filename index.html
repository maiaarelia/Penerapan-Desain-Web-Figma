Founder

Yang Hyun-Suk

Yang Hyun-suk (born December 2, 1969) is a South Korean music mogul, musician, and record producer. He is best known as a member of Seo Taiji and Boys in the 1990s. After the group disbanded, he founded and became the CEO of one of South Korea's largest record companies, YG Entertainment.
